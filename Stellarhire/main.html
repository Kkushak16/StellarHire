The Broken Placement Process

The current system is a maze of confusion for everyone involved.

Disorganized Communication

Scattered PDFs and endless WhatsApp groups lead to missed opportunities and constant "chase cycles" for students.

Manual Overload

Placement cells are buried in spreadsheets and manual workflows, taking time away from actual career mentoring.

Lack of Transparency

Institutions lack real-time data on placement metrics, making strategic decisions and reporting difficult.

A single source of truth for all stakeholders, designed for efficiency and clarity.
